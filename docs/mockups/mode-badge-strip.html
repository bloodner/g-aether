Which one feels right?

Each variation trades differently across density, personality, and visual weight. The winner is whichever you can see yourself looking at every time the app opens — without it ever becoming noise.

Readability Can you read the current state without thinking? At peripheral vision from 2 feet away?
Pressure Does it compete with the sensor cards below, or sit quietly as a frame?
Warning surfacing When services are running or you're on Turbo on battery, does your eye catch it immediately?
